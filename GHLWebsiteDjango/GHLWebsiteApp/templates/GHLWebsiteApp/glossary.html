Term Definition
SOG Shots on Goal - Shots that would go into the net if the goalie does not save them.
EA does not count deflections as shots on goal.
Shot Eff Shot Effectiveness - The amount of shots on goal divided by the total amount of shots taken, including blocked and wide shots.
PK Clears EA classifies this as pressing up on the RS while in the defensive zone on the PK, and the puck leaves the zone. Passes do not count.
